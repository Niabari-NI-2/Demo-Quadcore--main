ABOUT BESOT DEVCONSULT SERVICES

Besot DevConsult Services (BDS) is a group of experienced development practitioners providing social, agricultural, rural development and management consUltancy services to companies, non profit organizations, government agencies, multi-national companies, and individuals in and outside Nigeria. The company was formed to respond to a recognized need for quality capacity building of institutions (Public & Private), and development intervention agents and communities to engender desired sustainable development. BDS is registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) and presently, it has a strategic office located in the Federal Capital Territory and Asaba, Delta State.

BDS Vision

BDS is to be a foremost name in development consulting in Nigeria
with international recognition.

BDS Mission

Our mission is to facilitate people (male and female) oriented development
, maximize available resources (human, financial, technical and materials)
and ensure sustainable living.

BDS Objectives

The objectives of BDS are to:

  • Assist government and its agencies to provide practical development through facilitation of issues based governance
  • Build the capacity of communities to embark on self-motivated development programmes.
  • Render supportive services to development intervention agencies to help them achieve success in their desired areas of intervention
  • Empower the people (especially the rural poor), to be economically viable and productive in order to live above poverty through programmes and activities that will harness their collective ideas and efforts.
  • Render services geared towards ensuring sustainable environmental and natural resources management
  • Provide organizations development services to institutions and agencies to make them effective and efficient in their work.
  • Provide services that will promote food and livelihood security and increase income of the people.

BDS Programme Activities

The activities of Besot DevConsult Services include but not limited to the following areas

  • Designing and facilitation of workshops and training programmes Development of strategic plans
  • Development planning and policy implementation
  • Conduct of social surveys and researches
  • Project management and conduct of project monitoring and evaluation as well as impact assessments
  • CSO/NGO capacity building and management
  • Proposal writing and fund raising
  • Organizational development and assessment
  • Designing of management information systems
  • Conduct of business/enterprise management programmes
  • Issues based governance programmes
  • Agriculture and livelihood programmes
  • Community mobilization and project implementation
  • Participatory development training
  • Micro-credit management and service delivery training

BDS Staff

BDS has crop of veritable development professionals of various backgrounds who have acquired many years of practical field experiences at various levels. They have skills in various participatory methodologies such as RRA, PRA and PLA as well as in facilitating problem solving, reaching consensus, creating plans, needs assessments, designing and implementing projects, training of community development workers and managers of institutions and organizations. Many of them have worked in various management capacities with reputable organisations before joining the pool of Besot DevConsult Services. At various capacities the team members have rendered services to various governments and its agencies, organizations and programmes such as oil companies, nonprofit organisations World Bank, DFID, LEEMP, UNDP, coalition groups, local and state governments.
